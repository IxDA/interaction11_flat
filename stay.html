stay


Boulder has some great hotels with Rocky Mountain charm, with a range of prices and amenities. All designated “conference” hotels below have special IxDA rates and are served by our conference bus loop. View Interaction11 Boulder Hotels in a larger map

St Julien Hotel and Spa

Official Conference Hotel
900 Walnut Street

SOLD OUT We’ve contracted a set of great rates at one of the classiest joints in town – The St. Julien, our home base for the conference, includes a beautiful view and wonderful amenities. The hotel is at 9th and Walnut, right down the road from BDW. To reserve your room, call 877-303-0900 and when connected to Reservations, say you are with the Interaction Design Conference. You can also email reservationsmgr@stjulien.com and in the subject line put: Interaction Design Conference. The St. Julien is right on our conference bus loop.

Boulder Outlook Hotel and Suites

Conference Hotel
800 28th Street

ROOMS STILL AVAILABLE Independently owned in indie-minded Boulder, the Outlook is also Boulder’s first zero-waste hotel. Reasonable rates of $74-$104. Call (800) 542-0304 or (303) 443-3322 and ask for the Interaction'11 rate. The Outlook is also on our conference bus loop.

Boulder Creek Quality Inn and Suites

Conference Hotel
2020 Arapahoe Street

ROOMS STILL AVAILABLE The Quality Inn is just a stroll across campus to the conference venue, but further from downtown Boulder. Call 1-888-449-7550 and ask for the Interaction11 rate of $85/night. Our buses loop to the Boulder Creek Quality Inn.

Best Western Boulder Inn

Conference Hotel
770 28th Street

ROOMS STILL AVAILABLE The Best Western Boulder Inn is another option right off of campus. Call 800-233-8469 and reference code “IxDA” for the rate of $84/night. The Best Western Boulder is also on our conference bus loop.

Hotel Boulderado

2115 13th Street,

As one of the first Boulder hotels, our location in the heart of downtown is perfect for all types of vacations. The hotel's name comes from the words "Boulder" and "Colorado" so no guest would forget where they had stayed. The hotel has become a National Register Landmark and is a member of the National Trust's Historic Hotels of America. The Boulderama is also on our conference bus loop.

Boulder Marriott

2660 Canyon Boulevard

The Boulder Marriott is also on our conference bus loop.
